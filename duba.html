Accomodation

THE DUBA SUITE

It typically comprises two expansive bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom, connected by a communal lounge and dining area, making it ideal for families or small groups seeking an exclusive bush retreat. Large private verandahs extend from the living spaces, providing uninterrupted views of the productive floodplains where abundant wildlife, including the concession's famous lion-buffalo interactions, can often be observed
